You can create your own survey.
Each question will have a label and a helper with multiple choice answer.
For example:
label
How friendly is this survey generator?
answers
helper
please answer honestly
The number of answer is flexible and you can choose between:
  • checkbox (multiple choice)
    helper will be display here
  • radio (single choice)
    helper will be display here
  • input (free answer)
    helper will be display here
When multiple answers is choosen you have to assign a score to each answer.
When free answer is choosen you have to assign a score to each keyword you expect.
